About This Yearly Timetable System

At Flexora, we believe that effective planning is the key to a well-organized and impactful ministry. Under the leadership of our CEO, Hossana Korosi Maidoki, we developed this Yearly Timetable System to help ECWA Women Fellowship Ungwan Maidoki efficiently structure their programs and activities throughout the year.

Why This System?

Church agencies often struggle with scheduling, last-minute adjustments, and overlapping events. This system provides a clear, structured roadmap for the entire year, ensuring smooth coordination and better preparation for every gathering.

How It Works

  • Comprehensive yearly scheduling to outline all events and activities in advance.
  • Conflict prevention by ensuring no overlapping programs.
  • Easy access and updates for seamless adjustments when necessary.
  • Better participation and engagement by giving members ample time to prepare for events.

Our Mission

Through this system, we aim to enhance organization, promote participation, and reduce the stress of last-minute planning. Led by Hossana Korosi Maidoki, Flexora is committed to providing tools that simplify church administration, ensuring that every program runs smoothly and effectively.
